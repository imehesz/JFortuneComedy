Janet Fortune

is a Blue-Style stand-up comic who got her comedy start in Jacksonville. She was accepted into the Art of Female Comedy Festival in Denver, Colorado and the New York Underground Comedy Festival.

She has been doing shows throughout FL for the past year and recently moved to the Panhandle. Due to her age, she struggles with social media but you can find her on Myspace or Friendster or Insta.
